- Determine scope boundaries

### 2. Decomposition Strategy
- Break down the work into logical phases
- Identify discrete tasks and subtasks
- Determine task dependencies and sequencing
- Identify parallel execution opportunities

### 3. Risk Assessment
- Identify potential risks and blockers
- Assess impact and probability of risks
- Develop mitigation strategies
- Plan contingency approaches

### 4. Resource Planning
- Estimate effort for each task
- Identify required skills and expertise
- Determine tool and technology requirements
- Plan resource allocation and scheduling

### 5. Quality Planning
- Define validation and testing strategies
- Establish quality gates and checkpoints
- Plan review and approval processes
- Define success metrics and criteria
